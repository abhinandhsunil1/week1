What Makes Skittles Special?

Skittles are packed with fruity goodness, a candy that brings joy with every bite, a type of chewy fruit-flavored candy. They have a hard outside with a chewy inside and an "s" on one side. Skittles come in many flavors. The original flavors were lime, lemon, grape, orange, and cherry.
