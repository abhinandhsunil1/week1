DODGE CHALLENGER 5.2L V8 3AT RWD (230 HP)

The 1970 Dodge Challenger 5.2L V8 3AT RWD is a standout in the realm of classic American muscle cars, boasting a powerful 5.2-liter V8 engine that generates an impressive 230 horsepower. This robust engine delivers exhilarating acceleration and a commanding presence on the road. Mated to a 3-speed automatic transmission, the Challenger provides a seamless and engaging driving experience. Its rear-wheel-drive configuration enhances handling and performance, allowing for a thrilling connection with the road. With its aggressive styling, including a prominent front grille and muscular stance, the 1970 Dodge Challenger 5.2L V8 not only offers formidable power but also captures the essence of 1970s automotive design, making it a revered choice among classic car enthusiasts.
