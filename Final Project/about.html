WELCOME TO RETRO GARAGE

Here at Retro Garage, that legacy is alive and well when it comes to the Dodge Challenger — nothing says American muscle quite like this iconic car. This is our shop for offering a specific range of parts, accessories and memorabilia dedicated to enthusiasts' & collectors the same capturing that essence this iconic car.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to honor the Dodge Challenger’s storied history by offering high-quality, authentic products that enhance and preserve the legacy of this classic car. We are committed to delivering exceptional service and helping our customers find exactly what they need to fuel their passion for muscle cars.

OUR STORY

Retro Garage was founded by Abhinandh Sunil, a devoted car enthusiast with a particular passion for Dodge Challengers. Inspired by the beauty and performance of these classic cars, Abhinandh started Retro Garage with a vision to create a hub for fellow enthusiasts. What began as a personal project has evolved into a premier destination for all things Challenger.

WHAT SETS US APART

What makes Retro Garage unique is our deep expertise in Dodge Challengers and our dedication to quality. We carefully select every item in our inventory to ensure it meets the high standards that our customers expect. Whether you’re searching for rare parts or seeking advice on restoration, we’re here to provide you with personalized service and unparalleled knowledge.
