DODGE CHALLENGER

Few images are more matched to the "classic American muscle car" moniker than a 1970 Dodge Challenger, an embodiment of raw horsepower and in-your-face design from what many connoisseurs consider one of the greatest automotive eras. The 1970 Challenger was launched late in the model year as a Dodge response to mushrooming popularity of sleek, high-performance sporty/personal cars typified by Ford's Mustang. With its wide, low stance and a hood for days, along with short rear deck the XK exuded road presence. Under the hood, owners could choose from an assortment of powerful engines including the legendary Hemi V8 that delivered brisk acceleration and top-notch performance. The 1970 model year is especially revered for its eye-catching color selection and factory muscle car options, boasting top collector status amongst folks.
