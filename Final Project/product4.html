DODGE CHALLENGER 7.2L V8 4MT RWD (375 HP)

The 1970 Dodge Challenger 7.2L V8 4MT RWD is a pinnacle of muscle car engineering, boasting a colossal 7.2-liter V8 engine that produces a formidable 375 horsepower. This massive engine delivers unrivaled performance and aggressive acceleration, making it one of the most powerful and exhilarating vehicles of its era. The 4-speed manual transmission provides precise control and an engaging driving experience, allowing drivers to harness the full potential of this powerhouse. With rear-wheel drive, the Challenger offers exceptional handling and traction, contributing to its reputation as a high-performance icon. The 1970 Dodge Challenger 7.2L V8 stands out with its striking design, featuring a bold front grille and a commanding presence, solidifying its status as a highly sought-after classic among car enthusiasts.
