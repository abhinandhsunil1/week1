DODGE CHALLENGER 3.2L 3AT RWD (105 HP)

The 1970 Dodge Charger 3.2L 3AT RWD is a quintessential example of classic American muscle, renowned for its blend of power and style. Featuring a robust 3.2-liter inline-six engine, this Charger produces a respectable 105 horsepower, making it a reliable performer on the road. Paired with a 3-speed automatic transmission, it offers a smooth and enjoyable driving experience. The rear-wheel-drive layout further enhances its driving dynamics, providing balanced handling and a connection to the road that enthusiasts appreciate. With its iconic design, including a bold front grille and sleek, aerodynamic lines, the 1970 Dodge Charger 3.2L embodies the spirit of muscle cars from a golden era, making it a cherished model among classic car aficionados.
